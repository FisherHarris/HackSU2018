The Hunger Games
  • About

The Hunger Games is a web app designed as a take on the classic "Assassin" game. This game operates by having players update our mySQL databases with their location, and assigning each player a "target". Each player searches the area for his or her target until they can find and "assassinate" them. After an assassination occurs, the assassinated player's target then becomes the new target of the assassin. The game continues until only one remains. This game is powered by mySQL and PHP on the back-end and uses a bootstrap-powered front-end to display the game.

Created at Hack SU Spring 2018 --- Ben Targan, Fisher Harris, Thomas Chin
